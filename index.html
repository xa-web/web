Questions

Important lending details

Can I keep using my car?

Yes. For the standard auto-secured product, the borrower keeps using the vehicle unless a different structure is agreed.

Which documents are needed?

Usually identity document, IIN, phone number, and vehicle documents for auto-secured loans.

Is early repayment possible?

Yes. The interface is prepared for early repayment rules and schedule recalculation.
